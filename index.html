How We Make Money While Staying Unbiased
At Fairway Edge, unbiased analysis is everything. Our mission is to give you accurate, objective, data-driven golf predictions and equipment reviews — no hidden agendas, no corporate influence. We do this by keeping a strict wall between our editorial content and how we make money.
Our Editorial Independence
✓ We Buy Our Own Equipment
We don't accept free products from manufacturers. Every piece of equipment in our lab is purchased at retail, just like you would buy it. No cherry-picked review units. No special treatment. The product we test is the product you'd get.
✓ No Paid or Sponsored Reviews
Manufacturers, brands, and advertisers cannot influence our reviews, ratings, or predictions. Period. We don't let brands decide what we test. If a product scores a 6.2, we publish a 6.2 — even if that brand is an advertiser. Our testing team has zero contact with our revenue team.
✓ Non-Affiliated Professional Testers
Our equipment is tested by independent PGA professionals who are not affiliated with any brand. They have no financial relationship with the manufacturers whose products they test. Every test is conducted using TrackMan, GCQuad, or SAM PuttLab — verified, quantifiable data.
✓ Transparent Pick Tracking
Every prediction we make is logged publicly with a timestamp before the tournament begins. Our full win/loss record, ROI, and unit tracker is available on the Pick Tracker page. We show our losses as prominently as our wins. If our model has a bad week, you'll know about it.
✓ Open Methodology
We share how our prediction model works, what data sources we use, and how we calculate our scores. We want you to understand — and challenge — our process. Transparency isn't a marketing tactic for us. It's accountability.
Our Sources of Revenue
We keep a strict wall between our editorial and revenue operations. Our testing, predictions, and reviews are never influenced by how we fund the site. Here's exactly how we make money:
$ Affiliate Links
Our equipment reviews include links to retailers. When you click and purchase, we earn a small commission. These commissions never influence our ratings or recommendations. A product rated 7.0 stays 7.0 whether or not we have an affiliate relationship with the retailer.
$ YouTube Ad Revenue
Our YouTube channel earns revenue through standard ads. We don't create sponsored content or accept payment for positive coverage. The ads you see are served by YouTube, not placed by brands paying for favorable reviews.
$ Podcast Sponsorships
Our podcast includes clearly labeled sponsor reads. Sponsors are disclosed upfront and have zero influence over episode content, guest selection, or our analysis. If a sponsor's product comes through our equipment lab, it gets the same unbiased treatment as everything else.
$ Future: Premium Membership
Down the road, we may offer a premium tier with early access to picks, detailed model breakdowns, and exclusive analysis. Core predictions and equipment reviews will always remain free. If we launch this, we'll be transparent about what goes behind the paywall and why.
Your Trust Is Our Product
The moment we compromise our independence is the moment we lose everything that makes Fairway Edge worth visiting. We'd rather have a smaller, trusted site than a bigger one built on hidden deals. If you ever feel our content has been influenced by money, call us out. We take that seriously.

Our Editorial Independence

We believe editorial independence is the foundation of your trust. Every prediction, pick, and equipment review is driven purely by data and testing — never by who's paying us.

🛒

We Buy Our Own Equipment

Every product we review is purchased at retail, just like you would. Manufacturers don't send us cherry-picked units. We test what you'd actually receive if you ordered today. This eliminates any bias that comes from free product relationships.

🚫

No Paid or Sponsored Reviews

Manufacturers and brands cannot pay for a review, influence our scores, or decide what we test. If a product scores poorly, we publish that — no exceptions. Our community votes on what gets reviewed next.

🔬

Non-Affiliated Professional Testers

Equipment reviews are conducted by independent PGA professionals with no sponsorship ties to the brands being tested. They use calibrated launch monitors (TrackMan, GCQuad, SAM PuttLab) and standardized blind testing protocols.

📊

Transparent Methodology & Public Pick Tracking

We openly share our prediction model's methodology, test results, and every single pick we've ever made — wins and losses. Our pick tracker is public and timestamped. We never delete a bad week or hide a missed call.

🕐

Timestamped, Locked-In Predictions

All Wednesday predictions are published and timestamped before Round 1 tees off. We cannot retroactively edit picks. What we say on Wednesday is permanent — and you can hold us accountable every Monday in our public recap.
